Publishing game scores to leaguevine.com


If you would like to publish real-time game scores from iUltimate to leaguevine.com please follow these steps to ensure the scores are published correctly. Note that your iPhone/iPad must be connected to the internet to publish scores to leaguevine.com

We strongly recommend that you become familiar with iUltimate and leaguevine.com BEFORE you take scores for your first game. Feel free to use Fake League on leaguevine as test data to get comfortable. You can create new games and post scores to them while becoming familiar.

If you still have a question (or suggestion) send us a note.


I. Register at leaguevine.com
Posting scores to leaguevine.com requires you to register with leaguevine. IF you have not already done so, register with leaguevine.

II. Ensure the your team and the game(s) are setup on leaguevine.
For scores posting to leaguevine the team (and all players) must be created on leaguevine first. Likewise for games.

III. Create your team on the iUltimate iPhone/iPad app.
Choose your team on leaguevine.
  • Tap the Leaguevine tab on the Team view and follow instructions for picking your team.
  • When complete your team should look something like this:


IV. Add game(s) from leaguevine.
Pick your team and set pub level.
  • Choose Leaguevine as type.
  • Tap Vs. and follow instructions for choosing the game.
  • For publishing scores you must choose Scores for Leaguevine pub.
  • When complete your game should look something like this:


V. Your games scores will now be published to leaguevine while you are connected to the internet

If you are publishing to leaguvine you should see a blue message on the game's action view.
