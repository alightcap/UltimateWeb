Frequently Asked Questions

Click on a question below to get details:

How do I create my website for publishing the stats collected by iUltimate?
iUltimate automatically creates a custom site for your team the first time you upload your team. Each team you create get's it own site. The site is public so your and your teammates can visit the page without logging on.
On the "recent tweets" view I see "SKIPPED...TO MANY RECENT TWEETS". What causes that?
Unfortunately Twitter has limits to how many tweets it will accept from a user during the day. And worse, once it reaches those limits it rejects all messages for an hour or two. To keep you from exceeding your "tweet budget" iUltimate governs the tweets and skips tweets that are not considered critical.
On the "recent tweets" view I see "REJECTED BY TWITTER". What causes that?
This is usually the result of duplicate messages which Twitter rejects.
